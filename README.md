# 📊 Sales Data Analysis & Power BI Dashboard

## 📝 Project Overview
This project showcases an end-to-end Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of a sales dataset, spanning January 2003 to May 2005. The goal was to uncover insights into sales performance trends, product popularity, customer contributions, regional strengths, and discounting behavior.

The project is presented in two parts:
- A comprehensive **EDA report** (static analysis)
- An interactive **Power BI dashboard** (executive-facing visualization)

---

## 🔧 Tools Used
- **Microsoft Power BI** – for dashboard development and data visualization
- **Microsoft Excel** – for data cleaning and early exploration
- **DAX** – for calculated metrics and trend modeling

## 🎯 Key Insights
- **Sales trends upward**, peaking in Q4 and dipping in Q1 each year
- **Top 5 customers** (out of 92) contribute **over 21% of total revenue**, showing moderate customer concentration
- **Classic Cars, Vintage Cars, and Motorcycles** account for **67% of all orders**
- **USA, Spain, and France** drive **nearly 60%** of total sales, with the USA alone at 36%
- **92% of orders shipped successfully**; minimal dispute and cancellation rates
- **Large deals average a 28% discount**, vs. 10.8% for medium and 5.8% for small deals — confirming a volume-based pricing strategy

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights
The interactive Power BI dashboard includes:
- KPI Cards for Total Sales Value, Total Order, Average Monthly Sales

- Bar Charts for Customer and Product Contributions
- Deal Size vs. Discount % Breakdown
